Why Do We Need Stricter Regulations in Social Media?

Children as young as 5–12 years old are influenced by sensitive content. Nowadays, technology is a need, so more and more children are exposed to material that is not suited for their age. This can lead to depression, anxiety, addiction, and even insomnia.

Social media is a place of creativity and freedom, but it also allows the spread of inappropriate 18+ content. If not monitored, children may easily access these posts. Other harmful content includes unrealistic beauty standards, cyberbullying, online harassment, and false information that can negatively affect a child’s development.

Social Media's Safety: What Can You Do to Improve?

  1. Add stricter regulations to apps and websites.
    Stricter controls can help avoid harmful content and ensure children don’t get exposed to adult material.
  2. Post responsibly and think before you act.
    Predators and irresponsible content creators are one of the internet’s biggest dangers. Think before you post and avoid sharing adult content on platforms where children can access it.
  3. Take care of your children.
    Supervise their online activities, encourage educational content, and teach them healthy limits and media literacy.
